Fingerprint

A Secure HTTPS connection aids in increased privacy because it protects from others viewing your personal information like passwords, credit card information, and purchasing data.

The security is thanks to the advanced technology developed to employ unbreakable encryption for all connections. The issue with this new wave of privacy has created a loss of visibility

forcing schools and organizations to find a new way to monitor and filter information. The response to this “loss of visibility” into every detail of students' and employees' internet usage

is by developing new technology called “TTPS Proxy Appliances”. This technology gets around the guarantee of privacy that we once thought we had through an HTTPS connection.
